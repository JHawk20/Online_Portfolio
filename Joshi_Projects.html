Scratch Project

Computer Science Class

September 11, 2019

Click here to see documentation!

My partner, Zachary Kaatsmann and I have created a game that allows users to control a sprite through different obstacles. Multiple levels were implemented into the game to make the game more user-friendly. Level difficulties got harder as the game progressed, this kept many users to stay on the game. The hardest obstacle my partner and I had to face was the jump mechanics of the sprite, one great achievement that we accomplished was creating moving sprites as obstacles. Zachary and I had great communication skills, and worked together on both ends, coding and documentation. We also managed our class time really well and split our work evenly. Documentation was the hardest part about the project, coding was much easier because we understood the concepts of Scratch very well.
